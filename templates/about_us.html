About Us

RecycleDtrash is a website where one can find recycling bins all over the world, planted on a map. One can post public reviews and feedback, report about new bins and get into an enviornment-freindly community! By using RecycleDtrash you are reducing the accumulating garbage and pollution in our world, making a better living environment for you, your family and for everyone.
