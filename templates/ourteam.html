RecycleDtrash places recyclebins all over the city.Our weibsite gives the user information on wehre to find the nearest one, using a map planted with all our bins.

RecycleDtrash is a website where one can find recycling bins all over jerusalem, planted on a map. One can post public reviews and feedback. By using recycleDtrash you are reducing the accumulating garbage and pollution in our world, making a better living environment for you, your family and for everyone.
